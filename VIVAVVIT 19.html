QUERY CRACKING

Query cracking is a challenging task for all CSE/IT/MCA students which test the knowledge of building a query, breaking a complex query into a simple one, etc.

Registrations are done at online where the registration process was placed at the website vivavvit.com and spot registrations are also available before the event.

This event is designed for those who are really interested in working with the database.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:

  • This is an individual participation event where the participants solve the few complex queries related to database.
  • We prefer the usage of MySql.
  • Participants provided the database and the queries that are needed to be solved.
  • Queries with minimum complexity were given the preference.
  • The decision of the judges and the event head were treated as final and binding on all.

  • A screening test was conducted in span of 30minutes.
  • Screening test is for 3 members for a team. 
  • The team members may be of any department(CSE/IT/MCA).
  • The preferable team is one from 2nd year ,one from 3rd year and one from 4th year as the screening text consists of questions from all 4 years. 
  • Short listed students were promoted to the next round.
  • The decision of the judges and the event head were treated as final and binding on all.

Students from various colleges attended to take the test by using their mental ability to solve the questions.

POSTER PRESENTATION

 

This event gives the participants an opportunity to showcase their presentation skills by presenting on a topic to the panel of judges. It strives to explore the inquisitiveness of young minds by giving their incredulous ideas a definitive direction.

The decision of the judges and the event head were treated as final and binding on all.

Registrations are done at online where the registration process was placed at the website vivavvit.com and spot registrations are also available before the event starts.

RULES AND REGULATIONS:

  • Technical related topics must be chosen(Innovative topics are preferred)
  • Only two members per team are allowed.
  • Usage of power point presentation is not mandatory.
  • The maximum time given to each team is 7minutes.
  • Only participants who participate the preliminary abstract submission round were eligible for the final presentation.
  • Presentation will last for 10minutes.
  • Presentation skills, body language and other parameters are judged.

Participants participated in the event

The participants are allowed to register in the potential professor event through the website and they had submitted their abstracts of their respective topics with title and subject before the two days of the event.

The officers of the ACM VVIT GUNTUR Student Chapter shortlisted the students who are registered through mails based on the presentation of their abstracts. The shortlisted participants are intimated through mails and promoted to present their respective topics.

The faculty coordinators of this potential professor is M. Prashanth judged the students who presented their ideas. And the supporting faculty also joined the faculty coordinator for the judgment.

Dr.N.Sanjay Gandhi, Faculty coordinator for potential professor judged the students

After the presentations of all the students, the faculty coordinators take their time and finalize the two students from the participants and announced the results.

The students are awarded and certified with their performance and all the participants have gained their participation certificates issued by VVIT, Guntur and ACM VVIT Guntur Student Chapter.

DEBUGGING

VVIT has organized the VIVA-VVIT tech fest  with a cheerful atmosphere having attended by many students from the surrounding colleges. Students have participated in the events with enthusiasm and sportive nature and backed many prizes.


Banner at the event location

This year all the technical events conducted by ACM student chapter received a huge response from the students, with many participants from other colleges along with VVIT students. They have attended these events to compete with talent around them and also showcasing their own talents. These events bring competitive yet inspirational aura to the students.

DEBUGGING is an event for keen code observers and bringing out their inner tester talent in identifying the bugs and rectifying them within the stipulated time.

Students participating in the event

Rules and Regulations:

  • This is an individual event where the participants need to resolve the bugs of a pre-filled code in stipulated time.
  • The screening test was conducted based on the requirement.
  • Screening test consists of a single code with bugs and participant should solve in 20-30 minutes.
  • Final round should consist of a single complex program which should be solved in 30 minutes.
  • Judgment was based on the participant’s debugging skills along with the output which must match with required result. 

Student Coordinator & Faculty Coordinator examining the event

About 170 students have registered for this event and 150 students actively took part .Event was managed and coordinated by ACM student chapter members and faculty coordinators.

Students who have qualified the screening test are entered into final round. Under the supervision of student and faculty coordinators the best two accurate debuggers are considered the winner and the runner.
